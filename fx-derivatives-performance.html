Tableau 2023

FX & Derivatives Performance Intelligence

Transforming $2.8B in trading revenue visibility through executive dashboards that reduced reporting time by 75%

Tableau SQL Stakeholder-driven design Enterprise banking
01

Context

Organization Type

Global Investment Bank

Business Problem

Trading desks lacked real-time visibility into P&L performance across FX and derivatives products, leading to delayed decision-making and increased risk exposure during volatile market conditions.

Audience

Chief Risk Officer, Head of FX Trading, Portfolio Managers

02

The Analytical Challenge

What Was Unclear

Real-time P&L attribution across 50+ currency pairs and derivative instruments was scattered across multiple Excel spreadsheets and legacy reporting systems, with 24-48 hour delays in data availability.

Decisions Being Blocked

Risk managers couldn't make informed decisions about position adjustments during market volatility, and trading desks lacked visibility into which strategies were performing versus market benchmarks.

Data Complexity

Complex data integration across trading platforms, risk systems, and market data feeds with varying latencies, plus the need to handle both spot FX and complex derivative valuations in real-time.

03

Design & System Thinking

Metric Selection Logic

Focused on risk-adjusted returns and market impact metrics that directly tied to executive decision-making frameworks.

  • Risk-adjusted P&L - Combined revenue with Value-at-Risk measures to show true performance
  • Market Impact Ratio - Quantified slippage costs relative to benchmark pricing
  • Strategy Attribution - Broke down performance by trading strategy vs. market movement
  • Liquidity Metrics - Tracked bid-ask spreads and order book depth for decision context

Layout Reasoning

Hierarchical information architecture that prioritized risk and performance insights, with progressive disclosure for detailed analysis.

  • Top panel - Critical risk and P&L metrics visible at all times
  • Middle section - Performance attribution with drill-down capabilities
  • Bottom section - Detailed trade-level analysis accessible on-demand
  • Right sidebar - Real-time market indicators and alerts

Tradeoffs Made

Balanced real-time accuracy with system performance, prioritizing the most critical risk metrics over comprehensive coverage.

  • Real-time vs. Intra-day - Chose 15-minute refresh over true real-time to ensure data accuracy
  • Depth vs. Speed - Limited historical depth to 90 days to maintain query performance
  • Detail vs. Clarity - Aggregated complex derivative valuations to prevent cognitive overload
  • Coverage vs. Focus - Started with FX and key derivatives, deferring exotic products
04

Outcome

75% Faster Reporting

Reduced time-to-insight from 48 hours to 6 hours for end-of-day performance analysis, enabling faster strategic decisions.

$2.8B Revenue Visibility

Provided comprehensive visibility across all FX and derivatives trading activities for the first time in the bank's history.

95% User Adoption

Achieved 95% adoption rate among target users within 30 days, with dashboards becoming the primary decision-making tool.

"This dashboard transformed how we understand our trading performance. For the first time, we can see the true economics of our strategies in real-time, not just the revenue but the risk-adjusted returns that matter for capital allocation decisions."

— Chief Risk Officer, Global Investment Bank
